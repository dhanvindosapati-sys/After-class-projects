Facts About Motor Bikes

Motorbikes, also known as motorcycles, are two-wheeled vehicles that are powered by an engine. They are popular for their speed, agility, and fuel efficiency. Motorbikes come in various styles, including sport bikes, cruisers, touring bikes, and dirt bikes. They are used for commuting, racing, and recreational purposes. Riding a motorbike requires skill and safety precautions, such as wearing a helmet and protective gear.
